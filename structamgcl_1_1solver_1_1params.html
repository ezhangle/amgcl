unsigned amgcl::solver< value_t, index_t, interp_t, level_t >::params::coarse_enough

When level is coarse enough to be solved directly?

If number of variables at a next level in hierarchy becomes lower than this threshold, then the hierarchy construction is stopped and the linear system is solved explicitly at this level.
