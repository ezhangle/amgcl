Solves the linear system for the given system matrix.

Parameters
ASystem matrix.
PPreconditioner.
rhsRight-hand side.
xSolution vector.

The system matrix may differ from the matrix used for the AMG preconditioner construction. This may be used for the solution of non-stationary problems with slowly changing coefficients. There is a strong chance that AMG built for one time step will act as a reasonably good preconditioner for several subsequent time steps [3].
