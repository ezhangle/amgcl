Note
The prm argument is an instance of boost::property_tree::ptree class. The structure of the property tree should copy the structure of amgcl::AMG::params struct. E.g., one could
prm.put("coarsening.aggr.eps_strong", 1e-2);
Any parameters that are not relevant to the current AMG class, are silently ignored.
